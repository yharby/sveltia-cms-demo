## Getting Started

### 1. Edit Content

Click the **Content** menu and start editing your blog posts, pages, and settings.

### 2. Upload Images

Use the **Media** library to upload and manage images.

### 3. Preview Changes

See your changes in real-time before publishing.

### 4. Publish

Save your changes and they're automatically committed to GitHub!

## Features to Try

### Rich Text Editing

You can use **bold**, *italic*, ~~strikethrough~~, and even `code` in your content.
